Parking Information

Driveway parking is available in the small driveway in front of the attached garage.

Street parking alternative: Calendar parking is in effect year-round. Park on the same side of the street as the house (216) on even-numbered dates and move to the opposite side at 6 PM. On odd-numbered dates, park on the opposite side and move back at 6 PM.

Vehicles may be ticketed or towed if parked incorrectly or if blocking emergency or city maintenance vehicles. No posted signs indicate this rule, so please follow the schedule to avoid fines. If you have any questions, just ask!
